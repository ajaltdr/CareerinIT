    BSc CSIT in Nepal

    Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc CSIT) is one of the most demanding and trending course. The B.Sc CSIT program has been developed with a motive to provide the students with adequate knowledge in theoretical, practical and technical aspects to meet the ever-growing manpower requirement in the field of Information Technology in various Industries and Administrative fields. Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc CSIT) is a four years course program of Tribhuvan University (TU).

    The Bachelor of Computer Science and IT (Bsc CSIT) is a four-year, eight-semester program which is offered in the constituent and affiliated colleges under Tribhuvan University Institute of Science and Technology.

    It combines the study of software and hardware, and information and communication technologies, to provide students with the skills needed for a challenging and evolving career. For fulfilling the need of today’s job market in the field of IT profession which is one of the emerging careers and the program consists of many elective courses in total of 126 credit hours.

    In addition to the core computer science course and elective courses, the BSc CSIT program offers service course to meet the need of high technology applications that are the key requirements for modern industries and projects be it manufacturer or service Industries. The foundation and allied courses are designed to meet the need of undergraduate academic program requirements, and the service course is designed to meet the need of market demand and fast changing computer technology and application.

    Course Name: Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc CSIT)

    University: Tribhuvan University (TU)

    Duration: 4 years

    Academic Year: Semester

    Faculty: IT, Computing Studies and Technology

    Eligibility to Study BIT in Nepal:

    To be eligible to get admission for the Bsc CSIT course, the candidate applying for B.Sc.CSIT program:

    • • Should have successfully completed a twelve year of schooling in the science stream or equivalent from any university, board or institution recognized by TU.
      • You must have secured a minimum of second division in their +2 or equivalent.
      • Should have successfully passed the entrance examination conducted by TU securing at least 35% marks.
      • Compiled with all the application procedures.

    • (Note: Both Biology and Mathematics group of students of +2 level are eligible to apply for the course)

    BSc CSIT program comprises of the following courses ( Breakdown of credit hours of different courses under BSc CSIT )

       COURSES   CREDIT HOURS
           Computer Science Core Courses       75
           Natural Science Elective Courses        2
           Mathematics Courses       12
           English Courses        3
           Social Science & Management Courses        6
           Computer Science Elective Courses       15
           Internship and Projects        9
       Total Credit Hours   126

    BSc CSIT in Nepal and the future prospects:

    As we all know that the education field of Nepal is developing day by day. Many courses are being added to give a student the practical education rather than theoretical education. Information technology is one of them. This is the 21st century where the information technology is changing the way of life and changing the systems of many fields. To adopt this change by giving education the practical knowledge about changing information technology, many courses related to the computer science and information technology have been added to the faculties of different universities. Among them Bsc.CSIT (Bachelor of Science in computer science and information technology) is one which provides the knowledge of core computer science course as well as practical knowledge of information technology for innovative students.

    Among the many courses related to modern information technology and computer science, BSc CSIT is the course where one can gain knowledge about core computer science course as well as modern information technology. This course is designed to give knowledge about both computer science and information technology. This course is being run by the IOST (Institute of science and technology) under Tribhuvan University. Bsc CSIT is the four years semester based course which offers the most suitable and updated curriculum that fulfils the current industry needs meeting the global technology modernization. This course includes tough practical knowledge with project works and internship.
