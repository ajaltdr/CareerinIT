    BCA in Nepal

    Bachelors in Computer Application is a mixed type of technical course in Nepal. Pokhara University and Purbanchal University design and conduct the BCA program in Nepal currently. It includes a combined academic study of some engineering techniques as well as management skills within a single academic program.The students are required to have the technical knowledge of information technology in the process of operating and managing database administrations, system designing, analyzing, project managing,etc.

    Information of the BCA program in various Universities of Nepal.

    BCA at Pokhara University (Bachelor in Computer Application, PU)

    Academic Year of semester systems

    Time duration of 48 months

    The course details include:

    Semester I:

    • English I • Mathematics I • Digital Systems • Computer Fundamental And Application • Programming Logic and Techniques

    Semester II:

    • Business Communication • Mathematics II • Financial Accounting • Programming Language • Fundamental of Electrical and Electronics • Project I

    Semester III:

    • Object Oriented Programming • Data Structure And algorithms • System Analysis And Design • Financial Accounting Ii • Microprocessor

    Semester IV:

    • Numerical Methods • Visual Programming • Operating System • Database Management System • Computer Graphics • Project II

    Semester V:

    • Computer architecture • Networks And Data Communication • Web Technology • Java programming • Management Information System

    Semester VI:

    • Software Engineering • Statistics • Multimedia Technology • Web technology II • Project III

    Semester VII:

    • Electronic Commerce • Economics • Internship • Elective I

    Semester VIII:

    • Linux • Organization Management • Elective II • Project IV

    BCA in Purbanchal University(Bachelor in Computer Application, Purbanchal University)

    Academic Year of semester systems

    Time Duration of 36 months including 110 credits hours

    The course details include:

    Semester I (20 Credits):

    • Computer System Concepts • Digital Logic • Modern Business Practice • Mathematics I • Sociology and Professional Ethics • Digital Lab • Project I

    Semester II(17 Credits):

    • Microprocessor and Assembly Language • Hardware And System Installation • Mathematics II • Financial Accounting • Programming I • Project II

    Semester III(20 Credits):

    • Data Structures And Algorithm • Computer System Architecture • Database Systems • System Analysis and Design • Script Programming • Project III • Object Oriented programming

    Semester IV(20 Credits):

    • Computer Networking • Operating System • Probability And Statistic Theory • Numerical Methods • Visual And Windows Programming • Technical and Operation Management • Project IV

    Semester V(15 Credits):

    • Software Engineering • Web Technology • Object Oriented Analysis and Design • Advanced Programming • Computer Graphics • Apprentice Project

    Semester VI(15 Credits):

    • Management Information Systems • E-Commerce • Network Programming • Elective • IT Presentation • Apprentice Project II

    Eligibility to study BCA in Nepal:

    Both the Purbanchal University and Pokhara University Have the provisions to provide Admission to the students who have secured a minimum percentage of 45% in Aggregate or have passed in Plus Two or in Intermediate In Science, Commerce,Arts or any other disciplines

    Objectives of the BCA course:

    BCA programs consist of the core motives in production of highly qualified professionals who have both theoretical and practical knowledge in computer systems and its application.

    Several other motives behind are:

    • • Training for students to create solutions for information system based needs and problems in commercial,financial,governmental or other types of organizations.
      • Providing knowledge on various tools, concepts, and techniques of software development.
      • Preparation of students for acquiring careers in dynamic and continually evolving fields of software development.
      • Building up sound verbal and written communication skills.
      • Providing solid foundation for the further studies leading to MCA (Masters in Computer Applications), MIT, MS Computer Science and MBA.

    • Career Options and Scope of BCA in Nepal and abroad:

    Governmental Organizations, manufacturing Industry, information technology Commerce and education etc need a large volume of competent and skilled computer professionals where BCA students can get involved.
