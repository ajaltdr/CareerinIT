    Innovate Nepal Group


    “ING’s colleges partner with UK Universities and either offer their full-franchised programmes or an academic pathway offering their final year degree top-up.“

    As part of our expansion plan for a strong presence throughout Nepal, we have recently acquired/started 4 new colleges – Herald College (Kathmandu), Itahari International College (Itahari), Informatics College Pokhara (Pokhara) and Merryland College Biratnagar (Biratnagar). All these four new colleges are either already operational or are expected to commence their operation by Autumn 2016.

    Colleges

    Islington College

    ❮ ❯

    Islington College is Nepal’s premier Business & IT School which has been in operation since 1996. Formerly known as Informatics College, Islington College is the first academic institution in Nepal to partner with a UK University. Partnering with London Metropolitan University, Islington College offers, Undergraduate and post graduate degrees in IT and Business.

    Herald College Kathmandu(HCK)

    ❮ ❯

    Herald College Kathmandu is also an ING initiative. Located at Handigaon of Kathmandu, Herald College also aims at providing UK University IT & Business qualification to students.Herald College partners with iAcademy & University of Wolverhampton to provide 3-year BIT & 4-Year BIBM qualifications to the students. The campus was inaugurated by Prof. Geoff Layer, the Vice Chancellor of University of Wolverhampton on 3 April, 2016. This college came in operation from Autumn 2016.

    Merryland College Biratnagar (MCB)

    Merryland College Biratnagar (MCB) is the first UK University College in Eastern Region of Nepal established in collaboration with Merryland Higher Secondary School, Biratnagar, which is one of Nepal’s renowned high school. Partnering with iAcademy & University of Wolverhampton, MCB offers high quality UK university Business degree to the students of the Eastern Region. Currently, students from Central & Eastern Region of Nepal as well as North East India are pursuing their education.

    Itahari International College (IIC)

    Itahari International College (IIC) is the joint venture of Innovate Nepal Group (ING), Vishwa Adarsha College, Itahari & Sushma Godavari College, Itahari, established to provide UK University IT and Business degrees. IIC also partners with iAcademy & London Metropolitan University to provide 3-year BIT & 4-Year BIBM qualifications to the students. This college will come in operation from Autumn 2016.

    Informatics College Pokhara (ICP)

    Informatics College Pokhara (ICP), established in 2011 is an associate college of Innovate Nepal Group (ING). Initially, it started its academic journey as a franchisee of Informatics Education Limited, Singapore. Since 2017, Informatics College Pokhara has been directly partnering with London Metropolitan University, UK to provide enviable higher education in IT to students in Pokhara at an affordable price.
