    BIT in Nepal

    BIT in Nepal is one of best course in Nepal which directly deals with changing information technology. Over the years, it has been seen that BIT has been supplying the current need of IT industries. The bachelor in Information technology is an academic program comprising of core and advance IT unit. The core unit provides fundamentals of information technology that provides student the knowledge and skills in programming, system design, computer networks and communication. The advanced unit enables the students to exercise, develop and apply their knowledge and skills in many areas like multimedia, Artificial intelligence, mobile communication internet etc.

    BIT in Nepal has ability to change the current structure of information technology in Nepal. On the completion of this course one can face the problems of development and growth of information technology. Read more.

    Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology

    BSc.Cs.IT in Nepal

    Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc CSIT) is one of the most demanding and trending course. The B.Sc CSIT program has been developed with a motive to provide the students with adequate knowledge in theoretical, practical and technical aspects to meet the ever-growing manpower requirement in the field of Information Technology in various Industries and Administrative fields.

    The Bachelor of Computer Science and IT (Bsc CSIT) is a four-year, eight-semester program which is offered in the constituent and affiliated colleges under Tribhuvan University Institute of Science and Technology. Read more.

    Bachelors in Information Management

    BIM in Nepal

    Most of the students with management studies in their Intermediate level are seen interested in knowing about Bachelors in Information Management (BIM in Nepal). The popularity of this course is increasing in recent years. BIM is the Bachelors of Information Management. This is a four years bachelor program in Nepal. It is conducted by major Universities in Nepal in the semester system. This course program is a blend of the management skills and the IT knowledge preferably required in the management fields. The course program of BIM is designed in order to educate students to understand and then apply the theories and concepts of broad, integrated curriculum covering computing, informatics, business fundamentals and analytical decision-making. It generally seems to cover three fields of study within one ie. computing, business foundations and analytical methods. subject is also said to be the blend of the subjects BBA and BIT or BCA. Read more.

    Bachelor in Computer Application

    BCA in Nepal

    Bachelors in Computer Application is a mixed type of technical course in Nepal. Pokhara University and Purbanchal University design and conduct the BCA program in Nepal currently. It includes a combined academic study of some engineering techniques as well as management skills within a single academic program.The students are required to have the technical knowledge of information technology in the process of operating and managing database administrations, system designing, analyzing, project managing,etc. Read more.
